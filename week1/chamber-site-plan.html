Overview

Purpose

The aim of the Lubumbashi Chamber of Commerce is to promote business through member services, economic and community development and a public policy that balances economic prosperity with quality of life in the beautiful city of Lubumbashi. Disclaimer: All information presented here does not violate copyright © rights in any way. The purpose of this site is purely educational.

Audience

Any businesses in Lubumbashi who want to take thier businesses to the next level, and want grow their target market.

Join

This page contains information about the benefit of joining the chamber and a membership application form. There are three membership level options of non-profit, silver, and gold. The different levels have different perks including directory listings, publications, trainings, social media work, luncheons, sponsorships, advertising, etc.
